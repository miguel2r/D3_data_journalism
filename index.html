Current trends shaping people's lives.


The following graphs are showing some insights about the relationships of some of the most important factors that some demographics area in US are currently facing. The data is in percentages and by state. The data set included here is based on 2014 ACS 1-year estimates and was taken from https://factfinder.census.gov. The following variables were taken in account for this Data Analysis: Poverty, Age, Income, Healthcare, Obesity & Smokes.




Poverty vs Healthcare

Poverty has a lot of consequenses in a human life, and Healthcare is not the exemption. As individuals it is dificult to get a good Health condition if we can not pay for it. Goverments are trying to take care of this but now days the Healthcare System requires the contribution of the goverment and also of us as individuals to support the Healthcare System.

The graph above is showing a positive relationship between Heathcare and Poverty in percentage terms. We can observe some US states where the percentage of poverty is less that 12 % and the lack of Healthcare is around 4 %, this is the example of MA, were based on the graph the people that is living there got a good economy and because of it ,they might have good healthcare system as this data is reflecting.

On the other hand we can observe that if the poverty rate gets incresed, then the lack of Heathcare increase too.Therefore we can confirm that a bad economic situation lead into to a lack of Healthcare opportunities, as we observe in this graph the MS Mississippi State case.
